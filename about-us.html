Yummy Donuts

Yummy Donuts is an American multinational doughnut company. It was founded by Hanson Gregory in 1847.

Doughnuts are usually deep fried from a flour dough, and typically either ring-shaped or a number of shapes without a hole, and often filled, but can also be ball-shaped.
